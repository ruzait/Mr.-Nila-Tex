FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our banquet hall

What is the seating capacity of Al-Zakki Hall?

Al-Zakki Hall is spaciously designed to host large gatherings, with seating arrangements supported by 800 chairs and 100 tables provided for every event.

What events can be hosted at Al-Zakki Hall?

We host weddings, walima (lunch parties), conferences, official events, awarding ceremonies, competition events, birthday parties, and all types of celebrations.

What facilities are included with the hall rental?

Our hall includes stage lighting, sound system, bridal suite, prayer area, and parking for 50+ vehicles. 800 chairs and 100 tables are included with every booking.

What are your operating hours?

We are open daily from 8:00 AM to 10:30 PM. On Fridays, we are open for events except during Jumma prayer time.

How can I book Al-Zakki Hall?

Contact us via WhatsApp at +94 75 474 0232 or +94 77 775 5076 to check availability and receive a quote.

Is parking available?

Yes, we have parking space for 50+ vehicles available at the venue.
