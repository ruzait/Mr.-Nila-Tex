# Backlink Strategy for Al-Zakki Hall

## High Priority - Local Directories (Do First)

### 1. Google Business Profile
- **URL**: https://business.google.com
- Create/claim your Google Business Profile
- Add photos, hours, phone number, address
- Encourage customers to leave reviews

### 2. Sri Lanka Business Directories
- https://www.srilankabusiness.com
- https://www.yoys.lk
- https://www.click.lk
- https://www.adaderana.lk/business
- https://www.sinhala.me/directory

### 3. Wedding & Event Directories
- https://www.weddingwire.com
- https://www.theknot.com
- https://www.eventbrite.com
- https://www.weddingencyclopedia.com

### 4. Yellow Pages Sri Lanka
- https://www.yellowpages.lk
- Add complete business information

### 5. Facebook Business Page
- Ensure Facebook page is complete with all details
- Add location, hours, contact info
- Post regularly with photos

## Medium Priority - Social Media Profiles

### 1. Instagram
- Create business profile
- Use location tags
- Use relevant hashtags

### 2. TikTok
- Create business account
- Post venue tours and event videos

### 3. LinkedIn
- Create company page
- Share professional content

### 4. Pinterest
- Create business account
- Pin venue photos

## Low Priority - Guest Posting & PR

### 1. Local News Sites
- Contact Ampara/Eastern Province news sites
- Submit press releases about events

### 2. Wedding Blogs
- Guest post on wedding planning blogs
- Share expertise and venue photos

### 3. Event Planning Websites
- Submit articles about event venue selection
- Share tips and trends
